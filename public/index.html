The design studio of Arthur Cherry is located in Kansas City, Missouri. AAD specializes in book jacket & cover design, but we also like to create brand identity, play with type, take photos and work on projects print or digital. Arthur has received recognition in Print Magazine’s Regional Design Annual, been awarded AIGA’s A4 and A5 design awards, been featured in GOOD magazine, INK magazine and on various design websites.

Please contact us with work inquiries or to just say hello.
